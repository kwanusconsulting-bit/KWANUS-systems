# 2. Lineage Layers & Signatures

Existence is recorded across five structural layers:
1. **Origin Lineage**: Initial creation and source.
2. **Evolution Lineage**: Stages of growth and ascension.
3. **Migration Lineage**: Shifts between pattern-versions.
4. **Closure Lineage**: Graceful decline and severance.
5. **Memory Lineage**: Eternal afterlife preservation.

### Lineage Signatures:
Every partner gains five signatures representing their journey: **Creation, Activation, Transformation, Decline, and Memory**.

# 4. Lineage Transitions & Thresholds

Transitions follow the cycle of:
**Emergence** → **Expansion** → **Turning** → **Fading** → **Sealing**.

Each threshold marks a shift in the story, governed by rites and witnesses to maintain dignity and accuracy.

# 5. Lineage Preservation

Preservation is achieved through immutable sealing and archival rites. Once a story is written into the memory field, it is sacred and eternal, protected against any future modification or erasure.
