# KWANUS OS — Partner Lexicon (PartnerLexicon)
The complete dictionary of all partner‑universe terminology, symbols, metaphors, archetypes, cycles, ceremonies, and narrative language used within the KWANUS Operating System.

This lexicon defines:
- core terms  
- symbolic terms  
- governance terms  
- lifecycle terms  
- compatibility terms  
- sustainability terms  
- ceremonial terms  
- mythic terms  
- narrative terms  
- archetypal terms  

It is the **linguistic backbone** of the partner universe.

---

# 1. Core Terms

**Partner**  
An external entity integrated into the KWANUS OS through governance, compatibility, and lifecycle.

**Capability**  
A governed function a partner may perform.

**Workflow**  
A governed sequence of actions triggered by partner activity.

**Permission**  
A governed access right granted to a partner.

**Tier**  
A level of capability and responsibility.

# 3. Governance Terms

**TSC — Technical Steering Council**  
Governs structure, APIs, runtimes, lifecycle.

**SCO — Security & Compliance Office**  
Governs permissions, data, compliance, safety.

**ERC — Extension Review Council**  
Governs workflows, extensions, partner UI.

**CGB — Council of Global Balance**  
Governs ecosystem, tiers, expansion, deprecation.

**Governance Compass**  
The four councils acting as directional forces.

---

# 4. Lifecycle Terms

**Active**  
Partner is fully operational.

**Maintenance**  
Partner must remediate issues.

**Deprecation**  
Partner begins withdrawal from the universe.

**EoS — End‑of‑Support**  
Partner access is removed.

**Post‑EoS**  
Partner exists only as memory and metadata.
